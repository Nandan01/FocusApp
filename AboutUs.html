Our Mission

Our mission when creating this project was to help people stay organized. Introducing FOUCUS the best To-Do list and tracking application for all of your tasks at hand. We initially created this application for students to organized their work and keep track of what is due. However we realized that we can take it further and upscale it. FOCUS can be used in the work force for project management to make sure the team knows about all the deadlines and future due dates. Our team is made up of five highly motivated college students who are working to break into the tech field. Our goal for this app is to increase productivity for everyone because we all know what its like to be overwhelmed with work and forget what is there left to do.
