Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

What is Focus?


Focus is a life style brand whose "focus" is to keep anyone from students to the average working person organized. Everyone has things they need to get done but not everyone is organized in doing so. Focus offers a medium for us to take control of your day.


What Makes Us Different From The Competition?


Unlike our competition like Trello or Notion, only enterprise clients have to pay for our services. For everyone else, we won't bug you with subscriptions; our platform is free.


Does Focus Have Plans To Change The Free Tier?


No. For non-enterprise users the free tier is not subject to change for the foreseeable future.


What Features Can Users Expect In The Future?


Our next update will include a calandar view of all of your to-do list tasks. Multiple to-do lists will be able to be viewed in either a day, weekly, monthly or yearly calendar view. Users will also be able to push reminders for tasks to remind and keep them on task.
